Scrumboard

The Scrumboard is where all the Developers can see the Backlog Items in the current sprint, which is Sprint {{sprintNo}}. They can devide a backlog item in smaller task, and assign them to themselfes or other developers, and then put them in diffrent states according to the progress they have made.

A Backlog Item is done, as soons as all Story Points are used up and all Tasks are done.
